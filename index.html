Meet Sarah

Peninsula Dreamings

Sarah McIntosh is an abstract artist who uses the natural surroundings of her home land on the Mornington Peninsula as inspiration. The colours of land, sea and sky all present themselves in Sarah's paintings and represent the amazing diversity and beauty of the Mornington Peninsula.

Sarah studied a Bachelor of Fine Arts at Monash University and went on to study a Cert. IV in Graphic Design. Sarah has been a formally trained art teacher with 10 years experience working within a school setting.
